about me

I graduated from Atatürk University Civil Engineering Department in 2014 as a high honor student. I completed my master's degree with thesis in Geotechnical science at Eskişehir Technical University in 2023. I completed my military service as a Civil Engineer at Hakkari Provincial Gendarmerie Command. 6 years in the construction industry, Civil Control Engineer, Field engineer, etc. I have worked in various positions. Afterwards, then I decided to change careers and stepped into the software industry. In order to be successful in this sector, I participated in the Information Technologies Certificate Program given by METU Computer Engineering and this program is a compressed form of computer engineering. I have successfully completed this certificate program. I received the ISTQB Foundation Level, the most important certificate in the field of test engineering with international validity. In addition to this trainings, I try to get training, practice and meet the requirements of the software industry on different platforms (Udemy, Btk Akademi, Linkedin Learning, Hackerank, Patika.dev). I want to improve myself by working in a good company with my education and skills in the informatics and software sector and thus add value to the company I work for and myself. In my future career, I always aim to develop software that offers the easiest, highest quality, most effective and simplest solution by using up-to-date technologies. I am currently looking for a job.
